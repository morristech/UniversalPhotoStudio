PiCorner Simple Help

  • Slide right in the main photo grid page to show the main menus;
  • Slide left in the main photo grid page to show the secondary menu items;
  • In the main menu, click the menu group item to hide the menu items of this group;
  • In the photo slide page, click anywhere in the screen to bring up the action bar and the arc menu;
  • Click the '+' of the arc menu to bring up the child menu items; from left to right, they are 'share', 'like', 'set as wallpaper' and 'See details', you can also use the option menu when the action bar is shown;
  • When seeing the photo details, click the photo owner's avatar to see all his/her photos;
  • Click the photo comment to see all the photos of the user who made this comment;
  • In 'like user' page, click a user to see all his/her photos;
  • When viewing all the photos of a given user, click the 'home' button on the action bar to go back to the main photo grid page, sometimes you may just go too deep;
  • For some cases, the server just does not return the correct GEO location information of a photo, so the map page might not show the correct location;

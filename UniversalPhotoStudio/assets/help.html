Photo Corner Help

* Use your own instagram client. Instagram has some restriction on each client which access the instagram photos, for example, you cannot request more than 30 photos a page, and you should not use an unreasonable amount of bandwidth. So we're encouraging you use your own client.

To create your instagram client, you need:

  • Go to http://instagram.com/developer/clients/register;
  • Fill the form, put 'schema://upsigoauth' into the 'OAuth redirect_uri' text field;
  • After hitting the 'Register' button, you will be redirect to a new page with client id and client secret information;
  • copy the client id and secret information into the dialog text fields, then hit the 'OK' button;
  • From the main menu, sign into instagram again.

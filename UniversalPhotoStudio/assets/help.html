Help

  • Slide Right in the main photo grid page to show the main menus
  • Slide Left in the main photo grid page to show the secondary menus
  • In the main menu, click the group headers to show/hide the sub-items
  • In the photo page, click anywhere on the screen to show the action bar
  • In the photo details page, click the owner's avatar to see his/her photo stream
  • Click the photo comment to see the photo stream of the author
  • In the 'like user' page, click a user to see his/her photo stream
  • You can always go back to the main photo grid page by clicking the 'Home' button on the top left corner
  • When share a photo to other apps, the image url is on the clipboard, so you can paste them if needed
  • To comment 500px photos, you need to complete your profile in 500px web site first
  • To enable offline view for one of your flickr photo sets, click the button on the menu item, or long click on the menu item to bring up the setting UI;
  • It will take some time to download all your photos for offline photo sets, if you have new photos uploaded to a photo set, you can click Refresh button to get the new photo information;
  • Offline photo download will happen on time bases, or when you're charging your device, or when you're viewing your large photos. If you want to download photos now for a photo set, click the 'Download' button.

Known Issues

  • In some cases, the GEO location of the photos might not be correct, and result in a wrong place on the map
  • China mainland users might not be able to view photos thanks to the GFW

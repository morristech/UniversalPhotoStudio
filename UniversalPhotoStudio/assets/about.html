About

Thank you for purchasing PiCorner!

PiCorner is a photo viewer application for various popular photo sharing sites.

With this application, you can:
  • Browse awesome photos from: Flickr, Instagram, 500px, and more coming
  • Browse your own, your friends or anyone's phone stream of Flickr, Instagram and 500PX
  • Share photos
  • Download photos
  • View where the photos were taken on a map if the Geo location is available
  • Set photos as wallpaper
  • Flickr Specific Features:
    • Comment
    • Search
    • Tag Search
    • Offline view for your own photo sets
    • Friends' photos
  • 500px Specific Features:
    • Vote
    • Follow/Unfollow users
  • Instagram Specific Features:
    • Like/Unlike
    • Follow/Unfollow users
    • Nearby pictures
  • ...

During the development of this application, we leverage the following projects. We'd like to express our appreciations to the developers:

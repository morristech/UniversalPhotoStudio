About

Thank you for using this application, we really appreciate all your comments, suggestions, feature request and bug reports, you can always reach us here, leave issues there, or send us emails.

PiCorner is an open source photo viewer application, currently we support flickr, instagram and 500px, later we may support more photo sources. You can build the apk from our source code, if you got this application from google play store, we'll really appreciate.

With this application, you can:
  • Browse awesome photos from many photo sources;
  • See your own flickr and instagram photos;
  • Share photos to other applications;
  • Comment flickr photos of others;
  • Like/Unlike instagram photos of others;
  • Follow/Unfollow instagram users;
  • Set a photo as the wallpaper of your device;
  • ...

When developing this application, we used several open source projects, we'd like to express our appreciations to the developers:
  • Sliding Menu
  • jInstagram
  • ArcMenu
  • Android-ViewPagerIndicator
  • Universal Image Loader
  • TouchImageView

There will be more features coming, so please stay tuned.

Thank you.
